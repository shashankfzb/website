Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a driver when I'm too tired to drive?

Simply open the Otoddy app, set your pickup location, and choose "I'm too tired to drive" from the quick options. Set your preferred fare, browse available drivers, and select one based on their ratings and proximity. The driver will come to your location and safely drive you home in your own car.

How does fare setting work?

You set your own fare based on distance, time, and urgency. The app provides suggested rates, but you have full control. Drivers can accept your fare or negotiate. Higher fares typically get faster responses, especially during peak hours or bad weather.

How do I verify a driver before accepting?

Every driver profile shows their verification status, ratings, number of completed rides, and user reviews. You can also see their photo, driving experience, and any special certifications. All drivers undergo background checks and driving record verification before joining Otoddy.

What if I need to cancel my ride?

You can cancel anytime before the driver arrives with no charge. If you cancel after the driver has started traveling to you, a small cancellation fee may apply to compensate the driver for their time and fuel.

What are the requirements to become an Otoddy driver?

You must be 21+, have a valid driver's license for 3+ years, pass a background check, provide driving record, complete safety training, and have smartphone access. We also require references and a brief interview to ensure you're a good fit for our community.

How much can I earn as a driver?

Earnings vary based on location, hours worked, and demand. Most drivers earn ₹300-800 per hour during peak times. You keep 80% of the fare, with Otoddy taking 20% for platform maintenance, insurance, and support services.

What happens in case of an emergency?

The app has a built-in emergency button that immediately contacts local authorities and our 24/7 support team. We also provide real-time GPS tracking, emergency contacts notification, and direct line to emergency services. All rides are fully insured.

Is my car covered by insurance during the ride?

Yes, comprehensive insurance coverage is active from the moment the driver starts your trip until completion. This includes collision, comprehensive, and liability coverage that exceeds most personal auto policies.
